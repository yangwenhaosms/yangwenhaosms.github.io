About Me

I'm a 5th-year Ph.D. student in Center for Data Science at Peking Univerisity, advised by Zhihua Zhang. Currently, I'm a visiting student at Unversity of Alberta and working at Martha White's group. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University.

My research interests lie in probability and statistics. Now I mainly focus on developing statistical results for theoretical Markov Decision Processes.
