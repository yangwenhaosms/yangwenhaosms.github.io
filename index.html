About Me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in Department of Management Science and Engineering (MS&E) at Stanford University, hosted by Prof. Jose Blanchet.

Prior to that, I recieved my Ph.D. at Peking Univerisity in 2023, where I was advised by Prof. Zhihua Zhang. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University in 2018.

My research interests lie in intersection areas among probability, statistics and machine learning.

I will be on the 2024-2025 job market looking for full-time academia positions. Please feel free to reach out!
